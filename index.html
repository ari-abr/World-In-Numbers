Welcome!

Here you will find a variety of charts and graphs related to global topics:

    1) Population trends: change in population growth in various regions over time
    2) Demographics: population by race, religion, age, and other factors
    3) Economy: change in GDP, percentage of GDP spending, inflation

The original data is from Our World in Data and Wikipedia. Please note that there may be some slight inaccuracies among the data due to the possible difference in methods used to obtain and process it. The graphs and charts were made using Python and Python libraries, including Numply, Pandas, and Matplotlib.
